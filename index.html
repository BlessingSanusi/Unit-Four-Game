Cyrstal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will guess a specific amount if points to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score gets above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
